About

Focusin is a timer for mac that make use of the Pomodoro technique to help you getting things done and stay focused.

The Pomodoro technique works as follow:

25m
5m
4x
15m
Work
Short Break
Repeat
Long Break

If you want to know more about the Pomodoro technique, check the official website.

Focusin was created by Alberto Quesada and released under the MIT lisence.
